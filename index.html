About Me

Dedicated to Every Learner's Success

I am Christantos Aminga, a Special Needs Education (SNE) teacher passionate about making Physics and Chemistry accessible to all learners, including those with hearing impairment and autism.

My goal is to break barriers in education, inspire curiosity, and ensure that every learner has the tools and support they need to thrive through adaptive learning strategies and meaningful resources.
